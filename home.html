Welcome to Haibin Zhou’s homepage

https://yingqianwang.github.io/
Haibin Zhou received the B.S and M.Eng degrees from Hebei University of Technology, Tianjin, China,
in 2012 and 2015, respectively. From 2015 to 2018, he was an engineer of Zhuzhou CRRC Times Electric Co., Ltd. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the School of mathematics and computational science at Xiangtan, Xiangtan University, under the supervision of Prof. Y. Zhou.
His research interests include image processing and computer vision.
